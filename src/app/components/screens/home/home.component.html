About Us

Welcome to Jarek Farms, a Nigerian-registered company with a mission to improve essential food supplies for the people of Nigeria and to create employment and training opportunities for Nigerians. Our visionary group of Nigerian and UK nationals is committed to making a positive impact in Nigeria's agricultural sector. We have successfully launched our poultry business, and we are now expanding organically into other areas of agricultural need in Nigeria. Our goal is to help reduce national shortages of essential food products.

At Jarek Farms, we believe in sustainable agriculture that benefits both the environment and the local community. Our operations are designed to create employment and training opportunities for Nigerians, thereby supporting the country's economic growth. We are committed to excellence in all aspects of our business, from production to distribution. Our goal is to be a leading supplier of quality agricultural products in Nigeria and beyond. Join us on this journey towards a brighter future for Nigeria's agricultural sector.

Grains.
At Jarek Farms, we understand the importance of grains in Nigeria's food supply. That's why we're committed to increasing production and distribution of various grains, including maize, rice, and wheat. As a leading supplier, we provide grains for both human consumption and animal feed.
Fertalizers.
At Jarek Farms, we recognize the role of fertilizers in enhancing crop yields and improving soil health. As such, we offer a range of high-quality fertilizers to support sustainable farming practices in Nigeria. With our commitment to excellence and environmental sustainability, we aim to be the go-to supplier of quality fertilizers for Nigerian farmers.
Fresh and Frozen Chicken.
At Jarek Farms, we are proud to offer a diverse range of chicken products to meet the needs of our customers. Whether you prefer frozen or live chickens, we've got you covered. Our chickens are raised under strict conditions to ensure their health and well-being, resulting in delicious, high-quality meat.
Crated Fresh Eggs.
At Jarek Farms, we understand the importance of eggs as a nutritious and versatile food source. That's why we are dedicated to providing high-quality, fresh eggs to our customers in Nigeria. Our eggs are carefully handled and packaged in crates to ensure their freshness and integrity.
Fresh and Frozen Turkey.
At Jarek Farms, we are committed to providing a range of turkey products to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Whether you prefer frozen or live turkeys, we have you covered. Our turkeys are raised under strict conditions to ensure their health and well-being, resulting in delicious, high-quality meat.
Day Old Chicks.
At Jarek Farms, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, healthy chicks to our customers in Nigeria. Our day-old chicks are sourced from top-notch hatcheries and are carefully handled and transported to ensure their safety and well-being. We understand the importance of healthy chicks for successful poultry farming, and that's why we strive to provide the best quality chicks in Nigeria.
